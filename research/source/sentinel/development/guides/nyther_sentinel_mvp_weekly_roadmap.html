Core thesis — why it works
1
Network layer legitimate
Packets correctly formatted, authenticated, on-interval — Dvvalin sees normal traffic
2
Physics violated
Falsified voltages cannot satisfy S = V·conj(Y·V) — AEthelred residual spikes
3
Thermodynamic inconsistency
Hardware implants alter EM emissions — Brynhildr detects (Phase 2, post-funding)
✓
Attacker cannot fool all three simultaneously
